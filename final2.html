About Me:

Hi, I’m EJ McCabe, nice to meet you! I’m a 20 year old artist from Massachusetts, and this is the website for my comic! I’m currently attending the University of Connecticut as a Digital Media & Design major with a focus on Motion Design. In my spare time you can find me playing videogames, listening to music, drawing, or hanging out with my two cats, Ripley (named after Ripley from Alien) and Cody (named after Commander Cody from Star Wars).
