001 Bored

This is the first edition of The Adventures Of Figment And Clark, hope you guys like it! I figured I’d write a little blog-style caption under each comic to keep any readers up to date. This universe is something I’ve had in my head for years, so to finally start making it was a big deal for me. My biggest hope is for people to grow to love these characters as much as I do! Happy reading :)
