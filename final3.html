About The World:

Figment & Clark are just two characters from a larger universe that I’ve been imagining as early as 2017. They serve the Lich, the ancient embodiment of suffering from the underworld who was given physical form when it possessed the body of a dark mage trying to summon it. The Lich was thought to have been slain centuries ago, but it’s acolytes have risen again, creating portals to the underworld form which they harvest undead armies.

About The Characters:

Figment grew up as an outcast from the mage community, ostracized for his large vocabulary, short stature, and lackluster spellcasting skills. However, after being promised access to powerful magic should he join the forces of the Lich, he found his purpose in life. He particularly likes to levitate, obscuring his height with his long cape. He cares deeply for Clark despite his more buffonish moments.

Clark was raised by wolves in the mountains, only learning about human interaction in knight school (which his wolf parents afforded for him with stolen gold). Though he was kicked out of knight schol for clumsiness, he did soon met Figment, and they’ve been best friends ever since. Clark is kindhearted but not the brightest, often being corrected by Figment (though he only ever corrects him in the nicest way possible).
